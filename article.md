# CS 130 Project- Graduates Article

**This is the overview of the CS-130 Project.**

### Data Sets I Found Interesting
1. https://corgis-edu.github.io/corgis/csv/astronauts/
1. https://corgis-edu.github.io/corgis/csv/energy/

### Questions for Astronauts Data
1. Which occupations or backgrounds are most common among astronauts in the data?
1. What trends are visible in astronaut selection over different decades?

### Questions for Energy Data
1. How have residential energy prices changed from 1960 to now?
1. Which states showed the biggest increase in renewable energy use, such as wind or solar?

### How I Would Answer One of the Questions
1. To answer question 2 of the energy data set, I would first identify the columns related to renewable energy use, especially wind and solar, for each state. Then I would compare the earliest available year in the dataset to the most recent year for each state. For each state, I would calculate the change by subtracting the earlier renewable energy value from the later one. After that, I would sort the states by the size of the increase to find which ones had the biggest growth in renewable energy use.
